How It Works

1

Connect Credentials

Candidates securely link their profile to their educational institutions. Our system fetches verified credentials, degrees, and certificates directly from the source using the ONEST network.

2

Automated Aggregation

The Credex engine automatically aggregates these disparate records, validates their digital signatures, and normalizes the data into a unified, coherent skill profile.

3

Skill Graph Creation

We construct a dynamic Skill Graph for each candidate, calculating NSQF levels, proficiency scores, and recency metrics to provide a holistic view of their capabilities.

4

Instant Verification

Employers can instantly verify these skills using our secure portal or API. A simple QR scan reveals the full, verified history of the candidate, ensuring total trust.
